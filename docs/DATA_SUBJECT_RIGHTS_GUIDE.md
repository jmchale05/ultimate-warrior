# Data Subject Rights Request Guide

**Ultimate Warrior Challenges**  
**Your Rights Under GDPR and UK Data Protection Law**

---

## Overview

Under GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and the UK Data Protection Act 2018, you have several rights regarding your personal data. This guide explains how to exercise those rights in relation to Ultimate Warrior Challenges.

---

## Your Rights

### 1. **Right of Access (Subject Access Request)**

**What it means:** You can request a copy of all the personal data we hold about you.

**What you'll receive:**
- Your profile information (name, email, role, school)
- Consent records (timestamps showing when you agreed to our terms)
- If a student: your campaign results, year group, age, profile photo (if any)
- If a teacher/admin: classes created, deletion requests made or reviewed
- Any other data associated with your account

**How to request:**

**Option 1 – In the App (easiest for logged-in users):**
1. Log in to your account.
2. Click "Privacy" in the top navigation menu.
3. Click the "Download my data" button.
4. Your data will be downloaded as a JSON file to your computer.

**Option 2 – By Email:**
1. Send an email to **support@tuwc.online**.
2. Subject line: **"Subject Access Request"**
3. Include your email address or student ID and the school name.

**Timeline:** We will provide your data within **30 days** of receiving a clear request.

**Cost:** Free.

---

### 2. **Right of Correction (Rectification)**

**What it means:** You can request that we correct or update any inaccurate or incomplete data about you.

**Examples:**
- Your name is spelled incorrectly.
- Your age or year group is wrong.
- Your email address needs updating.

**How to request:**

Send an email to **support@tuwc.online** with:
- Subject line: **"Correction Request"**
- Your name or student ID.
- The school name.
- What information is incorrect and what the correct information should be.
- A reason for the correction (if relevant).

**Timeline:** We will respond within **10 working days** and make the correction within **30 days** (unless a legitimate reason prevents it).

**Cost:** Free.

---

### 3. **Right of Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten)**

**What it means:** You can request deletion of your data, with some exceptions.

**How it works:**

**If you are a student:**
1. Your teacher must submit a deletion request in the App (from the Campaigns page).
2. An admin will review the request and decide whether to approve it.
3. If approved, your data is deleted within 7 days.
4. If you or your parent/guardian believe the deletion is urgent, email **support@tuwc.online** with:
   - Subject line: **"Urgent Student Deletion"**
   - Your name or student ID, school name, and reason.

**If you are a teacher or admin:**
1. Email **support@tuwc.online** with:
   - Subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**
   - Your name, email, school name, and reason for deletion.
2. Support will verify your identity and check for any ongoing investigations or legal holds.
3. If no blocks exist, your account will be marked for deletion.
4. Your data will be removed within 30 days.

**Important exceptions:** We may not delete data if:
- A safeguarding investigation is ongoing.
- Legal proceedings involve your data.
- A school safeguarding record requires retention.
- A legitimate interest (e.g., preventing fraud) overrides your request.

If we cannot delete, we will explain why within **10 working days**.

**Timeline:** Deletion within **30 days** of approval.

**Cost:** Free.

---

### 4. **Right to Data Portability**

**What it means:** You can request your data in a machine-readable format so you can transfer it to another service or keep it yourself.

**What you'll receive:**
- All personal data associated with your account in JSON format.
- Structured and easy to parse by another system.

**How to request:**

**Option 1 – In the App:**
1. Log in.
2. Click "Privacy" → "Download my data".
3. Save the JSON file.

**Option 2 – By Email:**
1. Send an email to **support@tuwc.online** with:
   - Subject line: **"Portability Request"**
   - Your name, email, and school name.

**Timeline:** Within **30 days**.

**Cost:** Free. We cover the cost of sending the data.

---

### 5. **Right to Restrict Processing**

**What it means:** You can ask us to stop using your data for specific purposes while we keep it stored.

**Example:** While we review a complaint, you can ask us not to process your data until the review is complete.

**How to request:**

Send an email to **support@tuwc.online** with:
- Subject line: **"Restrict Processing"**
- Your name, email, and school name.
- A clear explanation of why you want processing restricted.

**Timeline:** We will respond within **10 working days**.

**Cost:** Free.

---

### 6. **Right to Object**

**What it means:** You can object to processing of your data for purposes based on our "legitimate interest."

**Example:** If we process your data to prevent fraud or run analytics, you can object.

**How to request:**

Send an email to **support@tuwc.online** with:
- Subject line: **"Objection to Processing"**
- Your name, email, and school name.
- A clear explanation of why you object and what processing concerns you.

**Timeline:** We will respond within **10 working days**.

**Cost:** Free.

---

### 7. **Right to Lodge a Complaint**

**What it means:** If you believe we have violated your data protection rights, you can file a complaint with the supervisory authority.

**Contacts:**

**If you are in the UK:**
- **Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)**
- Website: https://ico.org.uk/
- Email: casework@ico.org.uk
- Phone: 0303 123 1113

**If you are in the EU:**
- **Your National Data Protection Authority** (e.g., CNIL in France, BfDI in Germany)
- Find your authority: https://edpb.ec.europa.eu/

**Timeline:** You can complain at any time; there is no deadline.

## How to Submit a Request

### By Email

Send your request to: **support@tuwc.online**

Include:
- Your name and email address.
- Your school name.
- The type of request (e.g., "Subject Access Request", "Deletion Request").
- A clear description of what you are requesting.
- Any supporting documents or context.

### In the App

Log in and click "Privacy" to access data export, privacy notice, and contact information.

---

## Verification

For security, we will verify your identity by:
- Checking that the email matches the account on file.
- Asking you to confirm your school name.
- For sensitive requests, asking for additional proof (e.g., a photo ID).

## Timeline Guarantee

| Request Type | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Subject Access Request | 30 days |
| Correction Request | 30 days |
| Deletion Request | 30 days (if approved) |
| Portability Request | 30 days |
| Restrict Processing | 10 working days (response) |
| Objection | 10 working days (response) |

If we need more time due to complexity, we will notify you within the deadline and explain why.

---

## Fees

**All data subject rights requests are free.** We do not charge fees, except in rare cases where a request is manifestly unfounded or excessive, in which case we will notify you before processing.

---

## What if Your Request is Denied?

If we cannot fulfill your request, we will:
1. Notify you within the deadline.
2. Explain the reason for the refusal.
3. Provide information about your right to complain to the ICO.

Common reasons for refusal:
- Ongoing safeguarding investigation.
- Legal hold or litigation.
- Your request is manifestly unfounded or excessive.
- Balancing your rights against a legitimate interest we or another party has.
