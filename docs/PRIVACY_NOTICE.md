# Ultimate Warrior Challenges – Privacy Notice

**Last updated: May 2026**  
**Version: 2026-05**

## 1. Overview

Ultimate Warrior Challenges ("the App") is a web-based fitness campaign platform for schools. The App processes personal data belonging to teachers and students to administer fitness campaigns, track student progress, manage school access, and handle administrative requests.

**Data Controller:** Ultimate Warrior Challenges, pending confirmation of the responsible legal entity  
**Contact Email:** support@tuwc.online

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

### For Teachers and Admins
- Email address
- Display name
- Role (teacher or admin)
- School assignment
- Account creation date
- Consent records (version and timestamp)
- Profile photo (optional)
- Student authority consent records

### For Students
- Display name (entered by teacher)
- Age (optional, entered by teacher)
- Year group (Year 7–11)
- Student profile photo (optional, uploaded by teacher)
- Fitness campaign results (distance miles logged)
- Campaign progress tracking
- Class/year group assignment
- Consent records (entered by teacher on behalf of student)

### Automatically Collected
- Deletion request submissions (reason, timestamp, requester identity)
- Admin review decisions (status, timestamp, reviewer identity)
- School access codes (generated at school creation)
- Campaign start date (set when first teacher enrolls)

---

## 3. Why We Collect It (Legal Basis)

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

1. **Contractual Necessity:** To provide App access and campaign administration to schools.
2. **Legitimate Interest:** To track campaign progress, report results, and maintain audit trails for safeguarding.
3. **Consent:** Teachers confirm they have lawful authority and any required parental/school consent before entering student data. We record this confirmation in the App.
4. **Legal Obligation:** To comply with safeguarding requirements and respond to lawful data-subject requests.

**Important:** Teachers are responsible for confirming they have the necessary authority and consent from parents or guardians before uploading student data. The App does not verify these permissions.

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

- **Account Management:** To authenticate users, maintain school memberships, and route users to the correct interface.
- **Campaign Administration:** To assign students to classes, track campaign progress, and record fitness miles.
- **Reporting:** To generate school performance reports, student progress summaries, and completion statistics.
- **Deletion Management:** To process student deletion requests, provide audit trails, and notify admins for review.
- **Data Subject Rights:** To respond to access, correction, deletion, and portability requests.
- **Safeguarding:** To maintain records for audit and investigation purposes if required by school or local authority.

---

## 5. Retention and Deletion

### Student Data
- **Active Students:** Retained while the student is enrolled in the school and actively participating in campaigns.
- **Inactive Students:** After a student is removed via the App or leaves the school, their profile and results are permanently deleted once an approved deletion request is processed.
- **Deletion Requests:** When a teacher requests student deletion, an admin must review and approve the request. Once approved, the student profile and results are permanently deleted. A minimal audit record of the request and decision is retained only as long as necessary for safeguarding and audit purposes.

### Teacher/Admin Data
- **Active Accounts:** Retained while the teacher or admin is employed and using the App.
- **Inactive Accounts:** After an employee leaves, any remaining account records are handled manually outside the App in line with safeguarding, audit, and legal obligations.

### Consent Records
- Consent timestamps and versions are retained for the duration of the data subject's involvement with the App and for an additional period where required for regulatory evidence.

### Deletion Requests and Admin Notes
- Minimal deletion audit records (request ID, pseudonymized student reference, requester/reviewer IDs, decision, and timestamps) are retained only as long as necessary for safeguarding and audit purposes.

---

## 6. Who We Share Your Data With

- **Within Your School:** Other teachers and admins at your school can access student names, years, and progress data as part of campaign administration.
- **School Leadership:** Headteachers and senior staff may view school-level reports and summary statistics.
- **Admins:** App admins can view all school, teacher, student, and deletion request data for platform management and safeguarding.
- **Firebase (Google Cloud Services):** The App runs on Google Firebase. Google may access aggregated, anonymized usage data to provide the service and improve platform reliability. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Email Service:** Deletion request notifications are sent via email through Firebase's email extension. Email addresses of admins are shared with this service.
- **No Third Parties:** We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or other purposes.

---

## 7. Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
- Data at rest in Firebase is encrypted by Google.
- Access is restricted by role-based security rules (teachers see only their school's data, students see only their own records, admins have full access).
- Firebase firewalls and Google Cloud security measures protect the infrastructure.

**You are responsible for:** Keeping your account password secure and not sharing login credentials.

## 8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and UK data protection law, you have the right to:

### **Right of Access (Subject Access Request)**
You can request a copy of all data we hold about you, including stored results, consent records, and deletion requests.
- **Timeline:** Within 30 days of request.
- **How:** Email support@tuwc.online with "Subject Access Request" in the subject line.
- **In-App:** Logged-in users can download a JSON export of their data from the Privacy page within the App.

### **Right of Correction (Rectification)**
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **How:** Contact support@tuwc.online with details of what needs correcting.
- **Timeline:** We will respond within 10 working days.

### **Right of Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten)**
You can request deletion of your data, subject to our retention obligations and exceptions for safeguarding.
- **Students:** Teachers initiate a deletion request in the App, which an admin must review and approve.
- **Teachers/Admins:** Contact support@tuwc.online with a deletion request.
- **Exceptions:** We may retain data if required by law, for safeguarding, or if a legitimate interest overrides your request.

### **Right to Data Portability**
You can request your data in a portable, structured format (e.g., JSON) suitable for transfer to another service.
- **How:** Use the "Download my data" button in the App (for logged-in users) or email support@tuwc.online.

### **Right to Restrict Processing**
You can request that we stop processing your data, except for storage.
- **How:** Email support@tuwc.online with a justification.
- **Timeline:** We will respond within 10 working days.

### **Right to Object**
You can object to processing of your data for legitimate interest purposes.
- **How:** Email support@tuwc.online with your objection.
- **Timeline:** We will respond within 10 working days.

### **Right to Lodge a Complaint**
If you believe we have violated your data protection rights, you can lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority:
- **UK:** [Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)](https://ico.org.uk/)
- **EU:** [Your National Data Protection Authority](https://edpb.ec.europa.eu/)
